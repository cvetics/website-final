About

Terra Vida Design has been working with clients all across Dublin to design, maintain and build their dream gardens.
Our resident horticulturalist, Natalija Cvetic, is a skilled indoor and outdoor designer. Her work is inspired by nature and spirituality, and she uses her experience to transform her client's homes and gardens into beautiful escapes, vast expanses of colour, places to entertain or places to grow.
